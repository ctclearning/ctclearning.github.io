Why Focus on P3 to P6?

Because tuition should be purposeful — not premature.

Our Belief

At Crack the Code Learning, we coach only upper primary students — those in Primary 3 to Primary 6 — and this is a deliberate choice. We believe the early years of primary school are a time for children to enjoy learning, adapt to school life, and develop social-emotional skills.

We align with MOE’s view that P1 and P2 students should not be burdened with unnecessary academic pressure — that’s why there are no formal exams at those levels.

Why P3 to P6 Matters Most

  • Transition Years: P3 is when the curriculum becomes more rigorous. New components like Synthesis, Comprehension Cloze, and Heuristic Math begin appearing.
  • Exam Readiness: P5 introduces full-format exams, and P6 is the PSLE year — a crucial milestone in every student’s academic journey.
  • Mindset Shift: These are the years where students benefit the most from structured coaching that teaches them how to think, not just what to study.

It’s About More Than the PSLE

Our goal is not just to help students score well in PSLE, but to equip them with study strategies they can carry into secondary school and beyond.

"We’ve succeeded not just when students do well in exams — but when they take ownership of their learning."
