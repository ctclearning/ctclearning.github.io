Our Programmes

🚀 English (P3–P6)

English Made Simple, Strategic and Scoreable

At Crack the Code Learning, we believe English can be learned with clear strategies — just like Math or Science.

We understand that the PSLE English paper has many components — Grammar, Comprehension, Composition, Cloze, Oral, and more. Many students feel overwhelmed not because they lack ability, but because they lack a system. That’s where we come in.

Our CodeKey™ methodology gives students a structured, step-by-step way to approach every English question. Each component of the paper is broken down and taught with its own CodeKey™ — a thinking tool that helps students know exactly what to look for, how to think, and how to answer with precision.

Instead of relying on “gut feel” or memorisation, students learn:

  • ✅ How to spot clues in cloze and comprehension passages
  • ✅ How to apply grammar rules with confidence
  • ✅ How to structure compositions clearly and creatively
  • ✅ How to approach oral questions with depth and clarity

We also teach students how to think, not just what to write — so they become independent, reflective learners.

With our CodeKey™ system, learning English becomes systemised and structured, so scoring well becomes a predictable outcome — not a matter of luck.

🧠 The Metacognitive Learning Loop: Think. Monitor. Improve.

These powerful strategies are rarely taught elsewhere — but they make a real difference.
Research shows that students who are coached to reflect on their thinking consistently perform better in exams, and develop stronger confidence in tackling language tasks independently. [2]

➗ Math (P3–P6)

Crack the Code with Concept Mastery, Not Drilling

At Crack the Code Learning, we believe Math problem-solving isn’t about doing more — it’s about thinking better.

Many students struggle because they jump straight into solving without understanding the core idea. That’s why we use our signature CodeKey™ system — a step-by-step thought process that helps students unlock concepts before attempting a single question.

🧠 3 Key Steps to Crack Any Problem Sum:

  1. Extract & Understand the Keywords
    Spot what matters — quantities, relationships, comparison phrases.
  2. Link the Keywords to the Right Concept
    Is it a before-and-after, part-whole, or rate problem? We help students recognise patterns and match questions to the correct concept.
  3. Apply the Concept Correctly
    We teach students multiple strategies, so they’re never stuck with just one approach.
    “If this method doesn’t work, what else can I try?” That’s metacognitive thinking — and we coach them to do exactly that.

🔑 Our CodeKey™ in Action: The R.A.C. Method

  • 🔍 R – Read the question and highlight key information
  • 🧩 A – Analyse the concept and match it to the right strategy
  • ✅ C – Crack the problem with precision and confidence

🎯 The CTCL Difference

  • ✔️ We don’t drill endlessly — we teach smart classification of problems so students learn in clusters, not chaos
  • ✔️ We don’t rush into solving — we teach concepts first in ways your child can truly understand
  • ✔️ We don’t teach just one method — we give your child a full toolbox to use in any situation

With CTCL’s CodeKey™ approach, students become confident, adaptable thinkers — and consistently improve by 2–3 AL bands.

Meet Coach Asher

Hi, I’m Coach Asher, and I believe students shouldn’t just learn harder — they should learn smarter.

I was working as a manager in a multinational logistics company when a major health event changed my life — I underwent surgery and recovery from a brain tumour. During that time, I immersed myself in studying how the brain works and discovered how children learn most effectively.

This led me to the field of metacognition — the science of thinking about one’s own thinking. I found that when students learn how to pause, plan, and process information strategically, their performance skyrockets. That insight became the foundation of my teaching philosophy.

I left the corporate world to pursue a new mission: to help students unlock their full potential through cognitive coaching. At Crack the Code Learning, I now work with Primary 3 to 6 students to master English and Math using structured, brain-based strategies.

Many of my students improve by 2 to 3 AL bands — not by doing endless worksheets, but by learning how to break down questions, avoid careless mistakes, and study with clarity and confidence.

This isn’t just tuition. It’s next-gen coaching for the exam-smart generation.
