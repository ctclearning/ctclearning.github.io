📘 About Us

Who We Are

In today’s digital world, students face a growing number of distractions — short videos, mobile games, and constant notifications. These modern habits often lead to:

  • ⚡ Rushing through work
  • 🙈 Careless mistakes
  • 😵 Difficulty staying focused

That’s where we come in. At Crack the Code Learning, we don’t just tutor — we coach.

Our signature CodeKey™ system is designed to build students’ ability to focus, think metacognitively, and complete their work with precision and purpose. We empower students to take charge of their own learning journey.

How We Teach

Through our CodeKey™ methodology, we apply science-backed study techniques that are proven to boost long-term understanding and academic performance:

  • Spaced Repetition: Reinforcing knowledge over time
  • Active Recall: Strengthening memory through self-testing
  • Pomodoro Technique: Focusing in short, productive bursts
  • Feynman Technique: Explaining ideas in simple terms to check true understanding
  • Mind Mapping: Organising and linking key ideas visually
  • Interleaved Practice: Mixing topics for greater retention
  • Exam Paper Practice: Building exam confidence through real-style questions

These methods are seamlessly embedded into every lesson. It’s not just about what they learn — but how they learn.
