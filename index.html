About Us

At Crack the Code Learning (CTCL), we believe every child can succeed when taught the right way. Our structured, step-by-step approach helps students tackle English and Math with clarity and confidence.

Unlike traditional tuition, we coach our students using proven strategies, formula-based techniques, and engaging content that makes learning not only effective — but enjoyable. With personalised guidance and consistent feedback, we unlock each child's true potential.

Whether it's our proprietary CodeKey™ system for English or bite-sized mastery steps in Math, our goal is simple: help students crack the code to success, one concept at a time.

Our Programmes

  • P3–P6 English Mastery: Grammar, Vocabulary, Comprehension, Cloze, Composition & Oral
  • P3–P6 Math Mastery: STEP-by-step strategies and heuristics to build confidence and accuracy
  • Exam Prep: Weekly practice, mock exams, and targeted revision before major tests
  • Creative Writing: Learn to plan, structure and write engaging stories using the BRICK method

Meet Coach Asher

Coach Asher is a passionate educator who left his corporate career in logistics to pursue his true calling — helping students succeed. After recovering from a life-changing illness, he developed powerful, bite-sized strategies to help students understand complex topics with clarity.

With experience coaching hundreds of students, Coach Asher’s unique “Crack the Code” approach has helped students achieve AL1s, overcome learning anxiety, and build lifelong confidence in English and Math.

His philosophy? Every child can succeed — with the right method, mindset, and mentor.
