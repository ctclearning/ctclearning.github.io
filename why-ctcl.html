Why CTCL?

Crack the Code to Success

Metacognitive Coaching for Primary 3–6 English & Math

Cracking the Code vs. Traditional Learning

  • Traditional Learning: Focuses on repetitive practice and memorization. Success often relies on spotting familiar patterns rather than true understanding.
  • Cracking the Code: Teaches students how to uncover hidden rules and strategies, so they can tackle even unfamiliar questions with confidence.

How Cracking the Code Works

  1. Understand the System: Spot patterns and apply our CodeKey™ strategies in English or Math questions.
  2. Apply the Code: Use step-by-step techniques to solve problems clearly and systematically.
  3. Achieve Mastery: Students gain the skills to think critically and adapt — not just rely on memorization.

More Than Just PSLE

We believe success isn’t measured by exam grades alone. Our goal is to help students take ownership of their learning journey — to become independent, confident, and self-driven thinkers prepared for secondary school and beyond.

“If students only do well in PSLE, we’ve done our job. But if they master the study strategies to manage their own learning, we’ve fulfilled our mission.”
